Our History

Rosales National High School




Rosales National High School in Rosales, Pangasinan was originally established as a public secondary school to serve local students. On June 19, 1965, it was officially converted into a national high school through Republic Act No. 4325, placing it under the Department of Education. Since then, the school has expanded its programs and facilities and now offers both Junior and Senior High School education.
